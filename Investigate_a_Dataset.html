# vis_hist function is used to visualize histogram
def vis_hist(_title,_xlabel,_ylabel,df_no_show_,df_show_):
    params = np.array([df_show_,df_no_show_])
    labels = ['Show','No-Show']
    plt.hist(params, label=labels)
    plt.title(_title)
    plt.xlabel(_xlabel)
    plt.ylabel(_ylabel)
    plt.xticks(np.arange(2),('No','Yes'))
    plt.legend()
    plt.show()

# function to find count number of days of week present in a dataframe. Sunday was not considered as above I have already
# cheked and there are only 6 unique value in the dataframe and sunday is not one of them.
def week_count(_df_weeks):
    _mon=_df_weeks.query('Day_Of_Week == "Monday"')['Day_Of_Week'].count()
    _tue=_df_weeks.query('Day_Of_Week == "Tuesday"')['Day_Of_Week'].count()
    _wed=_df_weeks.query('Day_Of_Week == "Wednesday"')['Day_Of_Week'].count()
    _thurs=_df_weeks.query('Day_Of_Week == "Thursday"')['Day_Of_Week'].count()
    _fri=_df_weeks.query('Day_Of_Week == "Friday"')['Day_Of_Week'].count()
    _sat=_df_weeks.query('Day_Of_Week == "Saturday"')['Day_Of_Week'].count()
    
    day_week_count=[_mon,_tue,_wed,_thurs,_fri,_sat]
    return day_week_count

Conclusions¶

Result of each Question -¶

Research Question 1 : After looking at the histograms of diabetes, hypertension, alcoholism and handcap it is visible that paitents having these ailments are very less in numbers as compared to paitents not having these ailment so it will not be right to conclude anything from them

Research Question 2 : In this question I calculated propotions for top 5 neighbourhoods with most no-shows, interestingly four neighbourhoods had no-shows approximatley equal to 20% except ITARAR which had its no show up equal to 26.3% highest among all the neighbourhoods

Research Question 3 : The histogram of both show up and no show up looks identical hence no correlation can be found between days of week and show/no-show up

Research Question 4 : The histogram of SMS recieved count and show up clearly shows that there no effect of sms on show up for appointment

Few points to be noted are as follows First, Although the neighbourhood with highest number of no-shows is JARDIM CAMBURI but the highest percentage of no-shows are in ITARARÉ about 26.3% no shows which is. Secondly on Tuesdays there is highest number of no-shows it reason can not be concluded fro data above. Third most of the people who do not show up for appointments do not have the four ailments given in the dataset.¶

After all these analysis I can conclude that none of the factors above, stand alone can not determine possiblity of a paitents' show or no-show we would require more factors, like distance paitents have to travel to reach the hospital, availability of doctors each day, etc. and a group of factors have to be used to determine probability of show-up.¶

Limitations -¶

1 We do not know answers of question like Why paitents' are not showing up even after a SMS reminder

2 Few neighbourhood have very high no-show up as compared to other we will require few more details to know why is it so.

3 Information of doctor's availabilty and details will also be vital to figure out reason for no-show
